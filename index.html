Internet Show Intro

Intro comissioned for an online show. Features rigidbody physics for the title drop, as well as some morphing animation for the cartoon face, achieved using curves. Finished off with composition and post-effects in Adobe Premiere

Level Design

Assorted assets and level design work. Requirements were low-poly meshes that would triangulate without losing definition, mostly backdrop, but also some active geometry. Meshes were then passed off to an artist for texturing and materials.
